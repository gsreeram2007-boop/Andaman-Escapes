Island Destinations

Each island has its own personality — from bustling Port Blair to the serene solitude of Neil.

Capital City

Port Blair

🌅 Best: Oct–May 📍 South Andaman

The vibrant gateway to the Andaman Islands — home to the historic Cellular Jail, the Anthropological Museum, and the bustling Aberdeen Bazaar.

Most Popular

Havelock Island

🌅 Best: Nov–Apr 📍 Ritchie's Archipelago

Radhanagar Beach — Asia's finest — and Elephant Beach's snorkelling paradise make Havelock the crown jewel of the Andamans.

Hidden Gem

Neil Island

🌅 Best: Oct–May 📍 South Andaman

Often called the "Vegetable Bowl of the Andamans," Neil is a tranquil retreat known for its natural coral bridges and intimate, uncrowded beaches.

Adventure

Baratang Island

🌅 Best: Jan–May 📍 Middle Andaman

A thrilling journey through the dense mangrove forests leads to otherworldly limestone caves and the legendary mud volcanoes of Baratang.

Frequently Asked

Answers to Your
Island Questions

Everything you want to know before planning your perfect Andaman getaway.

When is the best time to visit the Andaman Islands?
The ideal time to visit is between October and May, when the sea is calm and the skies are clear. December to February are peak season months with the most pleasant weather. Avoid June through September as the monsoon makes sea crossings rough and many activities are suspended.
How do I reach the Andaman Islands?
By Air: Direct flights operate from Chennai, Kolkata, Delhi, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad to Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair. The flight takes approximately 2–2.5 hours from Chennai. By Sea: Passenger ships depart from Kolkata, Chennai, and Visakhapatnam, but this takes 3–4 days and is recommended only for budget travellers with plenty of time.
What documents do I need to visit Andaman?
Indian nationals need a valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, Passport, or Voter ID) and a confirmed flight ticket. Foreign nationals require a valid passport and visa. A Restricted Area Permit (RAP) is automatically granted at the airport for 30 days for most foreign nationals (some restricted islands require additional permits). We assist with all permit requirements as part of our packages.
Are the Andaman Islands safe for solo travellers and women?
Absolutely. The Andaman Islands are widely regarded as one of the safest destinations in India. The local population is warm and hospitable. With our packages, a dedicated guide is always available, and we maintain 24/7 on-trip support for all our guests.
Can I customise a tour package for a group or family?
Yes! This is one of our specialities. We offer fully customised itineraries for families, couples, corporate groups, and adventure teams. Simply contact us with your group size, preferred dates, budget, and any special requirements — we'll create a personalised plan within 24 hours.
Is there mobile network and internet connectivity on the islands?
BSNL has the best coverage across most islands. Jio and Airtel work well in Port Blair and Havelock. Internet speed is improving but remains slower than mainland India — consider this a feature, not a bug! Most hotels offer Wi-Fi, though speeds vary. We recommend embracing the digital detox as part of the island experience.
